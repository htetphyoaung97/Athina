TESARION (FIRE IFRIT)
TYPE : HP
STARS:
OVERALL RATING : 3.6/5
  • Increases the Resistance of ally monsters by 41%.
  • Mega Smash

    ((Spd+210) / 0.7)%

    Attacks with magical powers and inflicts continuous damage for 2 turns with a 50% chance. The damage increases according to Attack Speed.
    • Lv.2 Damage +5%
    • Lv.3 Harmful Effect Rate +10%
    • Lv.4 Damage +10%
    • Lv.5 Damage +10%
    • Lv.6 Harmful Effect Rate +15%
  • Triple Crush

    190% x3

    Attacks the enemy 3 times with magic and weakens the Defense for 2 turns with a 50% chance for each attack. (Reusable in 3 turns).
    • Lv.2 Harmful Effect Rate +5%
    • Lv.3 Damage +10%
    • Lv.4 Harmful Effect Rate +5%
    • Lv.5 Damage +10%
    • Lv.6 Harmful Effect Rate +10%
  • Ancient Power (Passive)
    Increases the damage inflicted on enemies with harmful effects by 30% and makes the enemy oblivious for 2 turns with each attack. Passive skills aren't activated in oblivion state. [Automatic Effect]
